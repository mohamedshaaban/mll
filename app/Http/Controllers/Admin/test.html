Frequently Asked Questions

  • General Questions

    How can I know if the products available on worx.sa are genuine?

    We are authorized from manufacturer to sell every item available on the website and we deal only with main manufacturer directly or authorized suppliers.

    Where do I find my device unique number?

    We define a unique number for each device we install/serve. The number is printed or written by hand on sticker in front of the copier that has Hoshan/Worx logo.

    Where do I find the copier model?

    In front of your copier search for a number looks like (Bizhub C360i, C266, MXM-265V).

    How can I know the toner number for my copier/printer?

    You can use the tool available on the homepage the “Toner Finder” by inserting your copier/printer model/unique number.

    Or

    If you purchased the copier from worx.sa earlier, you can sign into My Devices page and select your copier/printer from the list and click on order consumables.

    Or

    You can insert your copier/printer model in the search bar on any page to see all the products/items related to this copier/printer.

    Or

    You can click on Toner page and use the filter on the side of this page to select your printer/copier model to see all the results related to the selected model.

    How can I know the best copier/printer for me?

    First you must know:

     

    • If your printouts will be all black or you need color as well
    • Number of users
    • Monthly print volume

     

    Then:

    Use the chat tool to talk to our customer care to guide you with the best copier/printer that suits your needs.

    Or

    You can send us your message from Contact us Page so our sales consultancy team will contact you to answer your questions and/or assign a meeting with you.

  • Service

    How can I request maintenance to my copier/printer?

    If you purchased the copier/printer from worx.sa earlier then you can go to My Devices page and select for your copier/printer and click on request a service.

    Or

    Get your unique number or brand and model then:

    • Chat with us using the chat tool to submit a request for you, or
    • Sign in and visit My Maintenace Requests page and create a new request, or
    • Fill and submit a new request from Maintenance Request page.

     

    Can I request maintenance even if I didn’t buy the copier/printer from you? How?

    We are an authorized service provider to selected brands in Saudi which are available in Maintenance Request page in which you can see all the products that we cover and place a maintenance request from the same page.

    How can I track a maintenance request?

    If you raised your maintenance request from worx.sa, then you can go to My Maintenace Requests page and get the update on the status of your request.

    Or

    You can chat with us using the chat tool and our customer care team will update you with the status.

  • Delivery

    Who delivers my order?

    We have a full team with all capabilities to deliver all products available on worx.sa in addition to partnerships with international logistics companies.

    How long does it take to receive an order?

    In general, we commit to deliver the order maximum by the next working day of the order date if it is processed.

    Can I combine many orders together?

    No, every order has its own number and tracking number. There is a chance of receiving many shipments for one order due to the products’ size, but it will all be in one tracking number.

    Can I edit the delivery address after I complete my order?

    Not from the orders page.

    Chat with our customer care using the chat tool or send us your request on Contact Us page.
